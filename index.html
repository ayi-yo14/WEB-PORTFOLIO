☆ About the Artist ☆






PJ CUEVAS
Bachelor of Multimedia Arts Graduate

I am a visual artist dedicated to transforming ideas, emotions, and everyday narratives into meaningful works of art. My creative practice spans traditional sketches, expressive paint strokes, bold graphic designs, and the meticulous craft of fiber art. Through these diverse mediums, I communicate perspectives that extend beyond words—blending intuition, technique, and imagination to bring each piece to life.

I believe that art is both a language and a continuous process of exploration. Every work I create reflects a deep sense of curiosity, discipline, and passion for visual storytelling.
